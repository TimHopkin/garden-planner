Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial period and won't be charged.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. For annual plans, we also offer invoicing options for businesses.

Is my garden data secure?

Absolutely. All your garden designs and personal information are encrypted and securely stored. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
