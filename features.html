Extensive Plant Database

Access detailed information on over 5,000 plants, including vegetables, herbs, flowers, shrubs, and trees. Each plant entry includes:

  • Growing conditions (sunlight, water, soil requirements)
  • Planting and harvesting times for your climate zone
  • Spacing requirements and mature size
  • Companion planting recommendations
  • Common pests and diseases with organic solutions
  • Expert growing tips and tricks

Our database is continuously updated with new varieties and the latest growing information from horticultural experts.

Visual Garden Designer

Create accurate representations of your garden with our intuitive drag-and-drop interface:

  • Design gardens of any shape and size with customizable grid cells
  • Visualize plant spacing and arrangement before planting
  • See realistic plant representations with mature size indicators
  • Create multiple garden designs for different seasons
  • Save and compare different layout options
  • Print detailed garden plans with plant lists

Perfect for planning vegetable gardens, flower beds, container gardens, and more.

Interactive Growing Calendar

Never miss important planting dates with our personalized growing calendar:

  • Customized to your local climate and growing zone
  • Visual timeline showing optimal planting, tending, and harvesting times
  • Email and mobile notifications for upcoming garden tasks
  • Succession planting recommendations to maximize harvests
  • Season extension planning for early and late crops
  • Track frost dates and temperature patterns

Our calendar helps you time your garden activities perfectly for the best results.

Smart Companion Planting

Improve your garden's health and productivity with intelligent companion planting:

  • Real-time compatibility feedback as you design your garden
  • Color-coded indicators for beneficial and problematic plant combinations
  • Detailed explanations of why certain plants grow well together
  • Pest-deterrent plant suggestions to reduce insect problems
  • Plant groupings that maximize growth and flavor
  • Intercropping recommendations for space efficiency

Based on traditional garden wisdom and modern horticultural research for better garden results.

Garden Mapping & Sun Tracking

Understand your garden's unique conditions for optimal plant placement:

  • Map your actual garden layout using satellite imagery
  • Track sun patterns throughout the day and seasons
  • Identify full sun, partial sun, and shaded areas
  • Mark existing structures, trees, and permanent features
  • Create custom microclimates within your garden
  • Plan for seasonal changes in sun exposure

Placing the right plants in the right spots is key to garden success.
