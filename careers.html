Why Work at GardenMaster

At GardenMaster, we're passionate about combining technology with sustainable gardening practices to help people create beautiful, productive spaces. Our team brings together experts in horticulture, software development, and user experience to build the world's best garden planning platform.

We're a remote-first company with team members across the globe, connected by our shared mission to make gardening more accessible and sustainable for everyone.
