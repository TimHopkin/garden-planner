About GardenMaster

Helping gardeners create beautiful, productive spaces since 2020

Our Story

GardenMaster began with a simple idea: make garden planning accessible to everyone. Our founder, Emily Chen, struggled to plan her urban garden effectively and couldn't find tools that combined ease of use with powerful features.

What started as a passion project in 2020 has grown into the leading garden planning software used by over 250,000 gardeners worldwide - from beginners with their first window box to professional landscape designers managing multiple client projects.

Today, our team of 28 horticultural experts, software engineers, and design specialists work together to create the most intuitive and comprehensive garden planning experience available.

Our Mission

"To empower people to create sustainable, beautiful, and productive garden spaces through accessible technology and education."

Sustainability

We believe in promoting sustainable gardening practices that work with nature, not against it.

Accessibility

Gardening knowledge and tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of experience level.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our software to include the latest gardening research and technology.
